FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Do I need prior experience with Power BI?
No experience needed! We have Basic, Intermediate, and Advanced modules for all skill levels. Complete beginners are very welcome.
How do I register for a session?
Simply click any Register or Contact button and reach us via WhatsApp. Our admin will guide you through the process and confirm your schedule.
Will sessions be recorded?
Yes! All sessions are recorded so you can rewatch at any time if you missed something or want to review the material again.
Can I get enterprise training for my whole team?
Absolutely. We offer flexible corporate training programs, both online and on-site, for a minimum of 10 participants. Contact us to discuss a custom curriculum.
What if I miss a session?
Don't worry — all sessions are recorded. For private mentoring, you can also reschedule with at least 24 hours notice.
